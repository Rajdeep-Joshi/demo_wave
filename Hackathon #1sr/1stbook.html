Over View :-

So the Srimad Bhagavad Gita means 'the song of the god'. When Arjuna was afraid of the war and was thinking to give up the war, Lord Krishna gave him his divine teachings. These divine teachings were compiled into a script by Rishi Ved Vyasa and came to be known as Gita.
